Egg Noodle Lasagna

Description

This recipe, provided by a user on a long-forgotten internet forum, is good and easy. :D

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz.) pkg. egg noodles
  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 jar Ragu (Homestyle)
  • 8 oz. shredded Cheddar
  • 8 oz. shredded Mozzerella
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan
  • 1 large container cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger; add Ragu. Cook noodles; strain.
  2. Mix noodles, Cheddar, Mozzerella, Parmesan, cottage cheese, and eggs. Stir well.
  3. Layer hamburger sauce with noodles (add sauce first).
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes or until browned golden on top.
  5. Serve with salad and garlic bread.
