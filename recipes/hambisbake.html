Hamburger Biscuit Bake

Description

This recipe, taken from a Bisquick box, will have all of your dinner guests shaking their biscuits.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 can condensed Cream of Vegetable soup
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Snipped parsley
  • 1 cup Bisquick mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef and chopped onion together until the beef is browned. Remove from heat and drain off fat.
  2. Stir the soup, oregano, and a dash of pepper into the ground beef. Heat until bubbling. Turn into a 8 1/4" x 1 3/4" cake dish.
  3. Mix the Bisquick mix with the milk. Spread over top of the hot meat mixture.
  4. Bake dish in oven at 450 degrees F for 15 minutes or until brown.
  5. Spread with the sour cream, and sprinkle with Parmesan and parsley. Bake 2 additional minutes or place under broiler.
