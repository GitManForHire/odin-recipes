Biscuit Hamburger Bake

Description

This recipe, taken from a hamburger cookbook, will have all of your dinner guests shaking their biscuits with delight.

Ingredients for Hamburger Mixture

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 can (10 1/2 oz.) condensed cream of celery soup
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves, crushed
  • Ground pepper

Ingredients for Biscuit Topping

  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup Bisquick
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • Snipped parsley

Steps

  1. In skillet, cook the ground beef and onion together until meat is browned. Drain off fat.
  2. Stir in soup, milk, oregano, and a dash of pepper. Heat to boiling. Turn into 8 1/4" x 1 3/4" ovenware cake dish.
  3. Add milk to the Bisquick. Stir with a fork forming a soft dough; beat 15 strokes. Spread over top of HOT hamburger mixture.
  4. Bake dish in oven at 450 degrees F for 15 minutes or until biscuit topping is browned.
  5. Spread top with sour cream, and sprinkle with Parmesan and parsley. Bake 2 minutes longer. Makes 6 servings.
